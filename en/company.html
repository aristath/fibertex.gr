The company and its history

George and Chris Christidis are the sons of Michael Christidis, who was one of the oldest weavers.

His children from a young age were spending most of their time in the factory in Corinth and the love of their father for production was broadcasted to them.

They continue to develop his work in a higher standard and thus, created FIBERTEX in Athens, a state of the art unit, developed from the expertise they gained through-out these years.

FIBERTEX is a sophisticated industrial plant that produces textiles and has the courage to stand up against the world power production of such gods from the third world countries.

The philosophy is simple, to produced bed line products such as pillows, quilts and mattress protectors with the use of the highest quality raw materials, without causing carcinogen substances and substitutes with respect to human health and the environment.
